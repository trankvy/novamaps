— — The Problem
Brand Archetype The Precision Guardian

Raw thermal data is noise.
Nova makes it intelligence.

A wildfire crew doesn't need a dashboard. They need to know where the ground is still burning — right now, on a phone, in the field.

Raw infrared feeds from drones are dense, noisy, and require a trained eye to decode. In an active fire zone, that analysis window doesn't exist. Every minute spent interpreting data is a minute a hotspot goes undetected.

Nova Maps eliminates that gap. AI processes the thermal feed in real time, flags hidden hotspots with confidence scores, and pushes an actionable map directly to ground crews. No training required. No guesswork.
